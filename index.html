About the devotion

A Catholic tradition of faith and trust in Mary’s intercession

The devotion to Our Lady Undoer of Knots began in Germany in the seventeenth century and is practised by faithful people around the world. Pope Francis helped spread this devotion throughout Latin America.

"Virgin Mary, Mother of beautiful love, Mother who never abandoned a child who cried for help, help me trust God’s plan for my life."

This guide was prepared to support nine days of prayer through clear reflections and traditional devotional prayers.
